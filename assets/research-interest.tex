\section*{Research Interests}

My research interest is in Machine Learning for natural language \& visual understanding and generation with emphasis on Multimodal \& Multilingual LLM Safety and Alignment. My focus is developing algorithmic solution or approach to address the risk posed by Multilingual Large Language Models (MLLM) by highlighting open-ended questions on Value Misalignment, Robustness to Attack, Misuse, Autonomous AI Risks, Agent Safety, Interpretability for MLLM Safety, Strategies to MLLM Safety in Practice, AI Governance and Policy for MLLM Safety.
